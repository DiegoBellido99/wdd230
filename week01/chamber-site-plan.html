Overview

Purpose

We are Lima chamber of commerce, where we connect local businesses to drive growth and prosperity. As your central resource in the business community, we offer a wide range of services designed to help you achieve your business goals. From networking events and trade shows to professional development programs and business advice, we're here to support you every step of the way.

Scenarios

What events will the chamber hold this month that promote business networking? "This month, the chamber will host several networking events for local business owners. These include weekly networking breakfasts on Tuesdays, a business lunch next Thursday, and a business collaboration workshop on Friday. You can find more details about these events in the "Directory" section."
Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?" "Contact information for our board of directors is available in the 'Join' section of our website. There you will find a detailed list of board members, along with their emails and phone numbers. If you need to contact any of them, do not hesitate to contact us.
What has been the population growth in the area? "For detailed information on population growth in our area, we recommend visiting our 'Discover' section. There you will find updated data on population growth in recent years, as well as projections for the future.".
